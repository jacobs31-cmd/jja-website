Why Lapeer residents choose an independent agent

Captive agents (State Farm, Allstate, Farmers) sell only their one company's products. As an independent agency we represent 35+ carriers including Michigan-based Citizens, Frankenmuth, Wolverine Mutual, Michigan Millers, plus national A-rated names like The Hartford, Liberty Mutual, Hanover, Hagerty, and Hiscox. We shop your coverage every renewal and switch you to whoever offers the best value at the time.

Common questions from Lapeer clients

Is your office in Lapeer?

No — we're in Lake Orion, about 35 minutes south. But we're licensed throughout Michigan and most of our work is done by phone, email, and online, so distance doesn't matter. We have many long-time Lapeer clients we've never met in person.

Do you write boat insurance for Nepessing Lake?

Yes — and the rest of Lapeer County's 159 lakes. Boats, jet skis, pontoons, fishing boats, personal watercraft — we have multiple recreational-watercraft carriers and can quote any of them.

I have a hobby farm in Lapeer County — is that different from a regular homeowners policy?

Yes. Hobby-farm coverage is a specialty product that handles outbuildings, livestock, and farm equipment that standard homeowners policies exclude. We have carriers that specialize in this for Lapeer County's many small farms.
