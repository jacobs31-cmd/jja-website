Common questions from Goodrich clients

Is your office close to Goodrich?

About 25 minutes south. Most of our work is done by phone, email, and online — most Goodrich clients we never meet in person and they're insured with us for years.

I have a rural property with a barn and several outbuildings — is that fully covered?

It can be, but you need to verify it. Standard homeowners Coverage B (Other Structures) often defaults to 10% of the dwelling limit, which is usually not enough for properties with multiple outbuildings. We make sure the limit matches what's actually on your property and that the carrier underwrites rural acreage well.

Do you do agribusiness coverage?

Yes — including hobby farms, small commercial farms, and ag-equipment coverage. We have specialty carriers for this market.
