Common questions from Grand Blanc clients

Are you really based out of Lake Orion?

Yes — but the entire state of Michigan is our service area. We have long-time Grand Blanc clients we've never met in person and they're insured with us for decades. Most of our work is done by phone, email, and online, which works well for Grand Blanc professionals with busy schedules.

Yes. We have carriers that specialize in homes valued $500K and up, including replacement-cost coverage that actually reflects current Michigan construction costs, plus riders for scheduled jewelry, fine art, and high-end electronics. Most stock policies dramatically underinsure these.
