Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Why use an independent insurance agency instead of going direct?

Direct carriers (GEICO, Progressive direct, etc.) and captive agents (State Farm, Allstate, Farmers) only sell one company's products. An independent agent like us represents 50+ companies, so we can shop your coverage every renewal and switch you when another carrier offers better value — without you having to call around or start over.

Do you serve clients outside of Lake Orion?

Yes. While our office is in Lake Orion, we’re licensed throughout the entire state of Michigan. We have clients in Oakland County, Macomb County, Wayne County, and across the state. Most of our work is done by phone, email, and online — you don’t need to come into the office.

How quickly can I get a quote?

A quick quote takes about 2 minutes online. For a full comparison across multiple carriers, expect to spend about 15 minutes providing details. We typically have quotes back to you within one business day — often same-day for personal lines.

What carriers do you work with?

Our personal lines carriers include Citizens, Cincinnati Insurance, Progressive, Frankenmuth, Foremost, Liberty Mutual, Wolverine Mutual, Michigan Millers, Hagerty, American Modern, AAA of Michigan, The Hartford, Hastings Mutual, Hanover, and Hiscox — plus more than 20 commercial markets.
