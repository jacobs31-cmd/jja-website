Common questions from Fenton clients

Do I have to come to your office in Lake Orion?

No. Almost everything is done by phone, email, and online. Most of our Fenton clients we never meet in person and they're happily insured with us for years. If you'd rather meet face-to-face, you're welcome at our Lake Orion office — but you don't have to.

Do you write boat insurance for Lake Fenton or Silver Lake?

Yes — and Lake Ponemah, Long Lake, Squaw Lake, Crane Lake, Lobdell Lake, and any other Genesee/Livingston County waters. We have multiple recreational-watercraft carriers and can quote everything from a fishing boat to a 30-foot pontoon to a wakeboard tower.

What does Michigan No-Fault auto insurance cost in Fenton?

Michigan auto premiums are among the highest in the country, but they vary widely by zip code, vehicle, driving record, and PIP medical limit. Fenton (48430) typically rates better than metro Detroit zip codes but slightly higher than rural Genesee. The 2020 PIP reform let drivers choose their medical limit ($50K to unlimited) — for clients with good health insurance, dropping to $250K or $500K often saves several hundred dollars a year.

I'm moving to Fenton — how do I switch?

Tell us your move date and we'll line up new auto and home coverage to take effect on closing day. We coordinate with your old carrier to cancel your existing policy so you don't pay double. The whole transition usually takes 30 minutes of your time, spread across one phone call and one email exchange.
