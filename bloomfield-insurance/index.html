Why Bloomfield residents choose an independent agent

Most local insurance offices represent only one carrier — State Farm, Allstate, Farmers. Common questions from Bloomfield clients

Are you a local Bloomfield insurance agent?

We're based in Lake Orion and have insured Bloomfield-area families and businesses since 1981. We handle policies throughout Oakland County and the surrounding communities — most of it by phone, email, and online, so you never have to come into an office.

What insurance do you write for Bloomfield residents?

Personal lines: auto, home, condo, renters, life, umbrella, boat, motorcycle, RV, ATV, classic and collector car, flood, and pet. Commercial lines for Bloomfield-area business owners: general liability, commercial property, commercial auto, workers compensation, BOP, professional liability, cyber, contractor coverage, and more.

Do you handle high-value and high-net-worth coverage in Bloomfield?

Yes — it's a core part of what we do here. For homes valued at $750K and up, significant jewelry, art, or wine collections, and families with substantial assets, we place coverage through high-net-worth carriers (Chubb, PURE, Cincinnati Private Client) that offer guaranteed replacement cost, agreed value on valuables, very high liability and umbrella limits, and concierge claims.
