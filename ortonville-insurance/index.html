Common questions from Ortonville clients

How close is your office to Ortonville?

About 15 minutes south on M-15. Most of our work happens by phone, email, and online though, so most Ortonville clients we never meet in person.

Do you write boat insurance for the Ortonville Recreation Area lakes?

Yes — Big Fish Lake, Davison Lake, Algoe Lake, and the other lakes throughout the recreation area's 5,400 acres. We have multiple recreational-watercraft carriers.

I have a barn or outbuilding on a rural property — is that covered?

Yes. Coverage B (Other Structures) on a homeowners policy covers detached structures like barns, pole buildings, garages, and outbuildings. We make sure the limit is appropriate for what's actually on your property — most stock policies underinsure this.
