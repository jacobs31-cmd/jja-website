What we write for Oxford-area families

Whether you're in the Village of Oxford, on one of Oxford Township's 50+ lakes, out in Addison Township, or anywhere along the Polly Ann Trail, we write the full lineup of personal insurance:

  • Auto insurance — Michigan No-Fault explained in plain language. Multi-vehicle households, teen drivers, the after-claim re-shop most agencies skip.
  • Home insurance — homes throughout 48370 and 48371. We know the local replacement-cost expectations from older village homes to newer construction in the Lakes of Indianwood and surrounding subdivisions.
  • Boat, jet ski, and pontoon — Oxford Township has more lakes than just about anywhere in Oakland County. Stony Lake, Lakeville Lake, Davis Lake, Crescent Lake, Tan Lake, and dozens of others (mostly former gravel pits). We have multiple recreational-watercraft specialists.
  • Classic and collector car — we're a Hagerty agency.
  • Motorcycle, RV, and ATV — Oxford-area toy coverage.
  • Life insurance — term, whole, and universal life.
  • Umbrella — extra liability over your auto and home. Especially important for lake-area households.
  • Flood, condo, renters, mobile home, pet — all available.

Common questions from Oxford clients

How close are you to Oxford?

Very. Our office at 4301 S. Baldwin Rd is just south of the Oxford Township border. We can be in downtown Oxford in 10 minutes. Most of our work happens by phone, email, and online though.

Do you cover the gravel-pit lakes in Oxford Township?

Yes — many of those 50+ Oxford Township "lakes" are former gravel pits that filled in, and they're surrounded by homes today. We write home, boat, and personal watercraft coverage for all of them.

I have a home on the Polly Ann Trail — is that any different?

From an insurance standpoint, no. Properties adjacent to rail-trails are treated like any other home. We've insured many Polly Ann Trail homes and know the carriers that price them well.
