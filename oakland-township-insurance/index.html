Common questions from Oakland Township clients

How close are you to Oakland Township?

Right next door. Our office at 4301 S. Baldwin Rd is in Lake Orion, which shares its western border with Oakland Township. We can be at your property in under 15 minutes if needed.

I have horses on my property — does my homeowners policy cover them?

Not adequately, for most policies. Personal liability for horse-related incidents (especially if you board for others) usually needs a specialty endorsement or a farm policy. Tack, equipment, and the animals themselves often need scheduled coverage. We have multiple carriers that specialize in equestrian properties.

My property is on 10+ acres with a barn and pole building — is that covered?

It can be, but the limits need to be set correctly. Standard Coverage B (Other Structures) defaults to 10% of the dwelling limit, which on a $750K home is only $75K — usually nowhere near enough for a barn plus a pole building plus other structures. We size limits to what's actually on your property.
